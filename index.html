    What is YouTube Shorts ?

    YouTube Shorts is a new short-form video feature from YouTube. You can Upload video under 1 minute to YouTube shorts for entertainment.

    How to Make YouTube Shorts Video ?

    To make a YouTube Shorts Video, follow the below easy steps.

    1. Open the YouTube app from your phone.
    2. Click on + Icon from the middle.
    3. Hit “Create a Short” button.

    How Youtube Dark Mode ?

    Tap on the profile icon in the top right corner. This will open a menu. Tap "Settings," then tap "General." Tap the "Dark theme" toggle to turn the mode on or off.

    When will youtube shorts be monetized ?

    Yes. Revenue sharing on YouTube Shorts started on February 1, 2023

    How much does youtube shorts pay ?

    Generally, creators can expect to make anywhere from just over $2 to around $20 or more per 1 million views for their Shorts.

    Does youtube shorts have ads ?

    Ads on Shorts are skippable video or image ads that show up between Shorts in the Shorts Feed.

    Best time to post shorts on Youtube ?

    The best time to post YouTube Shorts is between 6 P.M. & 10 P.M. on the weekdays and between 12 P.M. and 6 P.M. on the weekend.
